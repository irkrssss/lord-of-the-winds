RACE
PHILOSOPHY

Lord of the Winds is an opportunity to compete with riders from all over the world without leaving your home spot. You ride, upload tracks, and compete for real rewards in the overall ranking.

Where to ride?

LOCATION: WORLDWIDE

Any frozen water body or snow field.

Mandatory condition:
Flat snow or ice surface. Elevation difference not more than 2m per 100m distance.

Rewards

Complete the challenge in all disciplines of your class and earn the WINDLORD status (insignia and finisher medal).

CHOOSE MODE

GPS-SPEED

MODE 1

Determination of maximum speed on a 50-meter section. Calculated by the internal race algorithm using the formula V = 50 / t, where t = t2 - t1, taking time t1 and t2 from all uploaded activity files matching the participant's application type.

> SPEED LIMIT: REMOVED

MARATHON

MODE 2

Determination of the minimum time to cover 42 kilometers. Number of turns and trajectory do not matter. Time and distance are calculated from the start of the activity. Distance can exceed 42 km; the system will automatically record the time for 42 km. Activities under 42.000 km will not be counted.

> DISTANCE: 42.0 KM

FEDOR KONYUHOV

MODE 3

Determination of the maximum distance covered in a single heat. Trajectory does not matter. The activity must not be interrupted by recording pauses, and stationary time must not exceed 25% of the activity.

> STATUS: SURVIVAL

FORREST GAMP

MODE 4

Determination of the maximum total distance covered during the competition. Calculated as the sum of distances of the participant's activities from the moment of application submission until March 15th inclusive.

> DISTANCE: DEPENDS ONLY ON YOU
